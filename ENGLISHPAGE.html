TOPIC: LIFE AND WORK OF ILA SACHANI

Who is Ila Sachani?

Ila Sachani is a Kathiawar embroidery artist, a famous embroidery in Gujarat.

She was born on nineteenth of December in 1895 in Amreli, Gujarat. She did

embroidery art on many things such as on cusions, bedcovers, pillows, and more.

Ila Sachani faced a big challenge since she was born, as she had both of her hands

loosened. But that did not stop her from being what she wants to be.



How did Ila Sachani get famous?

Even though she did not have hands to use needles and make embroidery art, Ila Sachani did not

lose hope. Her mother and grandmother, who were skilled at Kathiawar embroidery stitching were

single-minded to help overcome her challenges. It was of course not an easy task, but Ila

accepted the challenge with determination. Already at a young age, she became expert at

stitching. Her younger brother and sister also helped and supported her. Ila knew many

other styles too, such as Kachhi, Kashmiri, Luckwani, and others.At first, she crafted

fine designs only for her family and friends. But as Ila's magnificient talent spread across

the village, people started admiring her for the work she did. Word reached the government office

in Surat.They decide to display some samples of her embroidery at a state exhibition.

Soon people observed the beauty woven by Ila's feet and readily purchased the creations.Ila

was no more a hidden talent, but famous! After this, she participated in more exhibitions,

at national levels. Ila Sachini, once a daughter of a farmer, who faced tough challenges,

worked hard and became a celebrated artist and received the President's Medal.

What can we learn from Ila Sachani?

Ila Sachani proved, through her creations, that challenges can be turned into victories with

focus, hard work, positivity, and that we should never give up. Her embroidery, created with

her feet, reminds us that art has the power to rise above physical boundaries and touch the soul.
